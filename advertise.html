Interactive advertising advice

1. Start with a strong headline

Use short, benefit-focused language that tells people what problem you solve and why you do it better.

2. Pick one clear CTA

Guide customers to the next step with action words like “Book now”, “Shop today”, or “Get help”.

3. Keep your message simple

Too much detail slows people down. Highlight the most compelling part of your offer, then let visuals do the rest.
